Our Commitment to COPPA Compliance

We’ve built COPPA compliance into every layer of our system. Here’s what that means in practice:

  • Automatic protection: When a child under 13 tries to register, our system immediately requires parental consent—this happens at the database level, so there’s no way around it
  • Parent control: Parents can access, export, or delete their child’s data at any time, and they can revoke consent whenever they choose
  • Data minimization: We only collect what’s truly needed to make the service work—no location data, no full names, no photos
  • Automatic cleanup: Voice conversations are deleted after 30 days, and emotional data is anonymized after a year
  • Security throughout: All data is encrypted both in storage and in transit, with comprehensive audit logging
