What We Do

We create Storytailor, a web platform where children create personalized stories at Storytailor.com. We’re also developing Story Intelligence™, a sophisticated AI engine that will power future versions of Storytailor and be licensed to partner platforms (similar to how OpenAI licenses GPT technology).

Our Commitment

At Storytailor, we’re committed to: - Following COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements fully - Protecting children’s privacy and putting their safety first - Being transparent about our data practices - Giving parents full rights and control over their child’s information - Maintaining strong security and data protection
