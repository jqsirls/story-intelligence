# Creating a Story on Storytailor

## Getting Started

Creating a personalized story on Storytailor is quick and magical. Here's how it works!

## Step-by-Step Story Creation

### Step 1: Access Story Creation
1. Click the **Create** button in the main navigation
2. Select **New Story**

That's it! You're ready to begin.

### Step 2: Choose Your Story Type

You'll be asked to select what kind of story you want to create. Storytailor offers 14 different story types, each designed for specific purposes and experiences.

**For Children (Ages 3-8+):**
- Adventure
- Bedtime
- Birthday
- Educational
- Financial Literacy
- Language Learning
- Medical Bravery
- Mental Health (Big Emotions)
- Milestones
- New Chapter Sequel
- Tech Readiness

**For Adults:**
- Inner Child (healing stories)
- New Birth (new parenthood)
- Child Loss (therapeutic grief processing)

*Want to know more about each type?* Check out our **Story Types Explained** guide for detailed descriptions of what makes each type unique.

### Step 3: Select or Create a Character

Every great story needs a great character! You have two options:

**Option 1: Use an Existing Character**
- Browse your character library
- Select a character you've already created
- They'll be the star of this new story

**Option 2: Create a New Character**
- Click **Create New Character**
- Follow the character creation process (see our Character Creation guide)
- This new character will be saved to your library for future stories too!

### Step 4: Let Storytailor Create Your Story

Once you've chosen your story type and character, Storytailor gets to work!

**What Happens:**
- Your personalized story is generated based on your selections
- The story is tailored to the age you specified for your character
- All content is age-appropriate and aligned with your chosen story type
- This usually takes just a few moments

### Step 5: Your Story is Ready!

**Automatically Saved:**
- Your story is automatically saved to your Library
- You can access it anytime
- It's ready to read, play, or share (based on your account settings)

**What You Can Do Next:**
- **Read or play the story** immediately
- **Create another story** with the same character
- **Create a new character** for more adventures
- **Browse your Library** to see all your stories

## Tips for Great Stories

**Mix and Match:**
- Try different story types with the same character
- Create multiple characters and give each their own adventures
- Use "New Chapter Sequel" to continue favorite stories

**Age Appropriate:**
- Story content automatically adjusts to your character's age
- Younger ages (3-5) get simpler language and shorter stories
- Older ages (6-10+) get more complex narratives and vocabulary

**Therapeutic Stories:**
- Medical Bravery stories can help prepare kids for doctor visits
- Mental Health (Big Emotions) stories help process feelings
- Adult therapeutic stories are designed with professional therapeutic principles

## Common Questions

**How long does it take to create a story?**  
Usually just 30-60 seconds! Your story will be ready to enjoy almost instantly.

**Can I edit a story after it's created?**  
Stories are generated as complete narratives. To create a different version, you can start a new story with different parameters or use "New Chapter Sequel" to continue the adventure.

**Can I use the same character in multiple stories?**  
Absolutely! That's one of the best parts of Storytailor. Create a character once and use them in endless adventures.

**Where do my stories go?**  
All stories are automatically saved to your **Library** where you can access them anytime.
