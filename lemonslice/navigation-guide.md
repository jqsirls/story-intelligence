# Navigating Storytailor

## Main Navigation

Storytailor's interface is designed to be simple and intuitive. Here's how to find everything you need.

## The Create Button

The **Create** button is your starting point for making magic happen.

**What You Can Do:**
- **Create → New Story** - Start creating a personalized story
- **Create → New Character** - Build a new character for your stories

This button is your gateway to creativity!

## Your Library

Click **Library** to:
- View all your saved stories
- Access your character collection
- Organize and manage your creations
- Replay your favorite stories

## Profile Image Dropdown

Click on your **profile image** (in the navigation bar) to access important account features:

### Subscription
- View your current subscription plan
- **Upgrade** your account if you haven't yet
- **Manage billing** for existing subscriptions
- Change or cancel your plan

### Get a Gift Card
- Purchase gift cards for friends and family
- Redeem gift cards you've received
- Share the gift of storytelling

### Account Settings
- Update your profile information
- Manage privacy settings
- Configure parental controls (for accounts with children under 13)
- Adjust notification preferences

### FAQ
- Quick access to frequently asked questions
- Helpful guides and tips
- Troubleshooting common issues

## Quick Navigation Tips

**To create your first story:**
1. Click **Create** → **New Story**
2. Choose your story type
3. Select or create a character
4. Let Storytailor work its magic!

**To find your stories:**
1. Click **Library**
2. Browse all your created stories
3. Click any story to read or play it

**To upgrade your account:**
1. Click your **profile image**
2. Select **Subscription**
3. Choose **Upgrade** and pick your plan

**To get help:**
1. Click your **profile image**
2. Select **FAQ**
3. Or email support@storytailor.com anytime
