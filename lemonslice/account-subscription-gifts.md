# Account, Subscriptions, and Gift Cards

Manage your Storytailor account, subscription, and gift cards all in one place.

---

## Accessing Account Features

### How to Get There
1. Click your **profile image** in the navigation bar
2. A dropdown menu appears with these options:
   - **Subscription** - Manage billing and upgrade
   - **Get a Gift Card** - Purchase or redeem gift cards
   - **Account Settings** - Update your profile
   - **FAQ** - Quick help and answers

---

## Managing Your Account

### Account Settings

**Access:** Profile Image → Account Settings

**What You Can Do:**

**Profile Information:**
- Update your name and email
- Change your password
- Update profile picture (optional)
- Set communication preferences

**Privacy Settings:**
- Control who can see your stories
- Manage sharing permissions
- Review data collection preferences
- Set content preferences

**Children Under 13 (COPPA Protections):**
- If you created an account for a child under 13, additional privacy protections are automatically in place
- Parent email is required and verified
- Enhanced privacy controls
- Restricted sharing features
- Age-appropriate content filters

**Notification Preferences:**
- Choose how you want to be notified
- Email notifications for new features
- Story completion notifications
- Account updates and newsletters

## Subscription Management

### Accessing Subscription Settings

**Access:** Profile Image → Subscription

### What You'll See

**Current Plan:**
- Your active subscription tier
- Included features and benefits
- Monthly or annual billing status
- Next billing date

**Billing Information:**
- Payment method on file
- Billing history
- Invoices and receipts
- Update payment details

### Upgrading Your Account

**If You Haven't Upgraded Yet:**

1. Click **Profile Image** → **Subscription**
2. Click **Upgrade**
3. Review available plans:
   - Features included in each tier
   - Pricing (monthly or annual)
   - Story limits and benefits
   - Special features per plan
4. Select your preferred plan
5. Enter payment information
6. Confirm your upgrade

**Benefits of Upgrading:**
- More stories per month (or unlimited)
- Advanced character options
- Premium story types
- Priority support
- Family sharing features
- Export and print options
- And more depending on tier!

### Managing Existing Subscriptions

**If You're Already Subscribed:**

**Upgrade to Higher Tier:**
- Review higher-tier plan benefits
- Upgrade anytime
- Pay only the prorated difference
- New features activate immediately

**Downgrade Your Plan:**
- Review lower-tier options
- Changes take effect at next billing cycle
- You keep access to current features until then
- Library content remains saved

**Change Billing Frequency:**
- Switch between monthly and annual
- Annual plans often include savings
- Changes apply at renewal

**Update Payment Method:**
- Change credit card on file
- Update billing address
- Ensure uninterrupted service

**Cancel Subscription:**
- Cancel anytime, no penalties
- Access continues through end of billing period
- Library may have limited access after cancellation (check terms)
- Can resubscribe anytime

## Gift Cards

### Accessing Gift Cards

**Access:** Profile Image → Get a Gift Card

### Purchasing Gift Cards

**Perfect For:**
- Birthday gifts
- Holiday presents
- Special occasions
- Sharing Storytailor with friends and family
- Giving the gift of storytelling

**How to Purchase:**
1. Click **Profile Image** → **Get a Gift Card**
2. Select gift card amount
3. Choose delivery method:
   - Email delivery (instant)
   - Printable gift card
   - Scheduled delivery (for future dates)
4. Add a personal message (optional)
5. Enter payment information
6. Complete purchase

**Gift Card Options:**
- Multiple dollar amounts available
- Can be applied to subscriptions or one-time purchases
- Never expire
- Can be combined with existing accounts

### Redeeming Gift Cards

**If You Received a Gift Card:**

1. Click **Profile Image** → **Get a Gift Card**
2. Look for "Redeem Gift Card" option
3. Enter gift card code
4. Click "Redeem"
5. Balance is applied to your account immediately

**What You Can Use It For:**
- New subscription
- Subscription renewal
- Upgrade to higher tier
- Extend current subscription
- One-time features (if available)

**Gift Card Balance:**
- View remaining balance in Account Settings
- Applies automatically to next billing
- Check expiration (most don't expire)

## Common Questions

### Account Questions

**Can I change my email address?**  
Yes! Update it in Account Settings. You'll receive a verification email at the new address.

**What if I forgot my password?**  
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it.

**Can multiple people use one account?**  
Account sharing policies vary by subscription tier. Check your plan details.

**How do I delete my account?**  
Contact support@storytailor.com for account deletion requests. Note that this may permanently delete your stories.

### Subscription Questions

**Can I upgrade mid-billing cycle?**  
Yes! You'll only pay the prorated difference, and new features activate immediately.

**What happens if my payment fails?**  
You'll receive an email notification. Update your payment method to maintain access.

**Do I get a refund if I cancel?**  
Refund policies vary. Check your subscription terms or contact support for specific cases.

**Can I pause my subscription instead of canceling?**  
Contact support@storytailor.com to discuss pause options.

### Gift Card Questions

**Do gift cards expire?**  
Most Storytailor gift cards don't expire. Check the specific card terms when purchasing.

**Can I use multiple gift cards?**  
Yes! Combine gift card balances on your account.

**What if my gift card code doesn't work?**  
Contact support@storytailor.com with your gift card code for assistance.

**Can I return a gift card?**  
Gift card return policies vary. Check terms at time of purchase.
