# About Storytailor

## What is Storytailor?

Storytailor is a personalized story creation platform where families create custom stories that feature their own characters and adventures. Whether you're a parent looking to create magical bedtime stories for your child, or an adult seeking therapeutic storytelling experiences, Storytailor brings your stories to life.

## Who It's For

- **Families with children ages 3-8**: Create age-appropriate adventures, bedtime stories, educational tales, and more
- **Adults**: Access therapeutic story types including inner child healing, new parenthood support, and grief processing
