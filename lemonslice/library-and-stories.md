# Your Library and Story Management

Your Library is where all the magic you've created lives! Here's how to access, organize, and enjoy your stories and characters.

---

## Accessing Your Library

### How to Get There
Click **Library** in the main navigation to access all your saved content.

### What's in Your Library
- **All Your Stories** - Every story you've created
- **Character Collection** - All the characters you've built
- **Story History** - Your complete storytelling journey

---

## Viewing Your Stories

### Story Display

**Browse All Stories:**
- Stories are displayed in an easy-to-browse format
- See story titles, featured characters, and story types
- Organized by most recent first (newest at the top)

**Story Information:**
Each story shows:
- Title
- Featured character(s)
- Story type (Adventure, Bedtime, etc.)
- Date created
- Quick access to read or play

### Filtering and Organizing

**Find Stories By:**
- **Character** - See all stories featuring a specific character
- **Story Type** - View all your Adventure stories, Bedtime stories, etc.
- **Date** - Browse stories by when they were created
- **Favorites** - Mark stories as favorites for quick access

---

## Reading and Playing Stories

### How to Enjoy Your Stories

**Reading:**
- Click any story to open it
- Read the complete story text
- Perfect for bedtime or quiet reading time

**Playing:**
- Many stories include audio narration
- Listen along as the story is read aloud
- Great for car rides, rest time, or when parents need a break!

**Replaying:**
- Stories can be enjoyed again and again
- No limit on how many times you can read or play a story
- Kids love hearing their favorite stories multiple times!

## Your Character Library

### Accessing Characters

All characters you've created are saved in your Library alongside your stories.

**View Characters:**

- Each character shows:

### Using Your Characters

**Create New Stories:**

- Use them in new adventures
- Mix multiple characters in one story

**Edit Characters:**
- Update character details
- Change personality traits
- Adjust appearance descriptions
- Modify as your child grows or interests change

**Character Story History:**
- See all stories featuring each character
- Track your character's adventures over time
- Build ongoing character development

## Common Questions

**How many stories can I save?**  
Storage limits depend on your subscription plan. Most plans allow unlimited or generous story storage.

**Can I delete stories?**  
Yes! You can remove stories from your library if you no longer want them.

**Can I print stories?**  
Depending on your subscription, you may be able to export or print stories for physical keepsakes.

**What happens to my stories if I cancel my subscription?**  
Check your subscription terms. Typically, stories remain accessible based on your account status.

**Can I share stories outside my family?**  
Privacy settings determine sharing options. Children's stories have strict privacy protections (COPPA compliance).

**How do I find a specific story?**  
Use the search and filter features in your Library to quickly locate stories by title, character, or type.
