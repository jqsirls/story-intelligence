## Common Questions

### Getting Started

**How do I create my first story?**
1. Click the **Create** button
2. Select **New Story**
3. Choose a story type
4. Select or create a character
5. Your story will be generated and saved to your Library!

**Do I need to create a character first?**  
You can create a character during the story creation process, or make one ahead of time. Either way works!

**How long does it take to create a story?**  
Stories typically generate in 30-60 seconds. You'll have a complete personalized story almost instantly!

---

### Characters

**How do I add a character?**
1. Click **Create** → **New Character**
2. Fill in the character details (species, name, appearance, traits)
3. Your character saves automatically to your Library

**Can I use the same character in multiple stories?**  
Yes! That's one of the best features. Create a character once and use them in endless adventures.

**How do I edit a character?**  
Go to your Library, find the character, and select "Edit." You can update any details anytime.

**Can I create characters that look like my child?**  
Absolutely! That's what makes Storytailor special. Customize appearance, ethnicity, personality, and inclusivity traits to represent your child authentically.

---

### Stories

**Where are my stories saved?**  
All stories are automatically saved to your **Library**. Just click Library in the main navigation to see them all.

**Can I delete a story?**  
Yes! You can remove stories from your Library if you no longer want them.

**How do I share a story with family?**  
Sharing options depend on your subscription plan and account settings. Check Library for sharing features, or contact support for help.

**Can I print stories?**  
Depending on your subscription tier, you may be able to export or print stories. Check your account features or contact support.

**What story types are available?**  
Storytailor offers 14 story types:
- 11 for children (Adventure, Bedtime, Birthday, Educational, Financial Literacy, Language Learning, Medical Bravery, Mental Health, Milestones, New Chapter Sequel, Tech Readiness)
- 3 for adults (Inner Child, New Birth, Child Loss)

For detailed explanations, see our Story Types Explained guide!

### Account Management

**How do I upgrade my account?**
1. Click your **profile image**
2. Select **Subscription**
3. Click **Upgrade**
4. Choose your plan and complete payment

**How do I change my subscription?**  
Go to Profile Image → Subscription to upgrade, downgrade, or change billing frequency.

**What if my child is under 13?**  
Storytailor is COPPA compliant! When you register a child under 13:
- Parent email is required
- Email verification is needed
- Enhanced privacy protections apply automatically
- Restricted sharing features protect your child's content

**Can I cancel anytime?**  
Yes! Cancel your subscription anytime with no penalties. You'll have access through the end of your billing period.

---

### Gift Cards

**How do I use a gift card?**
1. Click **Profile Image** → **Get a Gift Card**
2. Select "Redeem Gift Card"
3. Enter your gift card code
4. Balance applies to your account immediately

**Do gift cards expire?**  
Most Storytailor gift cards don't expire. Check the specific terms on your gift card.

**Can I buy a gift card for someone else?**  
Yes! Purchase gift cards through Profile Image → Get a Gift Card. You can send them via email or print them.

## Troubleshooting

### Story Creation Issues

**My story won't generate:**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Contact support if the issue persists

**The story doesn't match my character:**
- Double-check your character settings
- Make sure character details saved properly
- Try creating the story again
- Contact support with specific details

---

### Character Customization Help

**I can't find the ethnicity option:**
- Ethnicity is only available for **Human** and **Superhero** species
- Select one of these species types first
- The ethnicity field will then appear

**My inclusivity traits aren't showing in the story:**
- Traits are woven naturally into narratives
- Some traits appear more prominently in certain story types
- They're always respected in how the character is portrayed
- Contact support if you have concerns about representation

---

### Account Access Problems

**I forgot my password:**
- Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
- Check your email for reset instructions
- Follow the link to create a new password

**I'm not receiving emails:**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify the email address on your account
- Add support@storytailor.com to your contacts
- Contact support if emails still aren't arriving

**My payment didn't go through:**
- Verify your payment information is correct
- Check with your bank about declined transactions
- Try a different payment method
- Contact support for billing assistance

---
