## Novel Aspects (Preliminary Assessment)

### Novel Combination 1: Four-Pillar Integration
**Novelty**: Integration of four distinct intelligence systems working in concert for story generation.

**Prior Art Gap**: While individual components exist, the integrated four-pillar system appears novel.

### Novel Combination 2: Real-Time Multi-Agent Orchestration
**Novelty**: Real-time orchestration of 30+ specialized agents for story generation.

**Prior Art Gap**: Multi-agent systems exist, but real-time story generation orchestration appears novel.

### Novel Combination 3: Therapeutic Pathway Integration
**Novelty**: Evidence-based therapeutic pathways integrated into automated narrative generation.

**Prior Art Gap**: Therapeutic storytelling exists manually, but automated integration appears novel.

## Risk Assessment

### High Novelty Areas
- Four-pillar intelligence integration
- Real-time multi-agent story orchestration
- Therapeutic pathway integration in automated generation

### Medium Novelty Areas
- Age-appropriate narrative adaptation
- Quality assurance in automated generation

### Lower Novelty Areas
- Individual intelligence components (may be prior art)
- Basic story generation (well-established prior art)

## Recommendations

1. **Comprehensive Prior Art Search**: Engage patent attorney for comprehensive search
2. **Claim Narrowing**: Focus on novel combinations rather than individual components
3. **Provisional Filing**: File provisional to establish priority date
4. **International Consideration**: Consider PCT filing for international protection
