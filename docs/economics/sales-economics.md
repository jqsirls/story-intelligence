## CAC by Channel Breakdown

### PLG Free Tier (Product-Led Growth)

**Channel Description:** Self-service customer acquisition through free tier experience

**CAC Components:**
- Marketing: $5-10 (content marketing, SEO)
- Infrastructure: $2-5 (free tier support)
- Onboarding: $3-5 (automated onboarding)
- **Total CAC: $10-20**

**Conversion Flow:**
1. Free tier signup (low friction)
2. Story creation experience
3. Quality assessment
4. Upgrade to paid tier

**Conversion Rate:** 5-10% (free to paid)

**Sales Cycle:** 0-30 days (self-service)

**Code References:**
- `docs/business/product-led-growth.md` - PLG strategy
- `docs/business/unit-economics.md:272-275` - PLG economics

### Paid Advertising

**Channel Description:** Digital advertising (Google Ads, Facebook Ads, etc.)

**CAC Components:**
- Ad spend: $40-80 (CPC × conversion rate)
- Landing page: $2-5 (optimization)
- Attribution: $1-2 (tracking tools)
- **Total CAC: $50-100**

**Conversion Flow:**
1. Click ad
2. Landing page visit
3. Signup (free or paid)
4. Conversion to paid

**Conversion Rate:** 2-5% (visitor to paid)

**Sales Cycle:** 0-7 days (immediate conversion)

### Content Marketing

**Channel Description:** Blog posts, SEO, content creation

**CAC Components:**
- Content creation: $20-40 (writer, editor)
- SEO tools: $5-10 (monthly)
- Distribution: $5-10 (social media, email)
- **Total CAC: $30-60**

**Conversion Flow:**
1. Content discovery (search, social)
2. Content consumption
3. Website visit
4. Signup and conversion

**Conversion Rate:** 1-3% (visitor to paid)

**Sales Cycle:** 7-30 days (consideration period)

### Partnerships

**Channel Description:** Strategic partnerships and integrations

**CAC Components:**
- Partnership development: $100-200 (relationship building)
- Integration costs: $50-100 (technical integration)
- Co-marketing: $20-50 (shared marketing)
- **Total CAC: $150-350**

**Conversion Flow:**
1. Partner referral
2. Partner-branded experience
3. Signup through partner
4. Conversion to paid

**Conversion Rate:** 10-20% (partner referral to paid)

**Sales Cycle:** 30-90 days (partnership development)

**Code References:**
- `docs/business/partnerships/` - Partnership programs

### Referrals

**Channel Description:** Customer referral program

**CAC Components:**
- Referral incentives: $10-20 (discounts, credits)
- Program management: $2-5 (automation)
- **Total CAC: $12-25**

**Conversion Flow:**
1. Existing customer referral
2. Referred customer signup
3. Conversion to paid
4. Referrer reward

**Conversion Rate:** 15-25% (referral to paid)

**Sales Cycle:** 0-14 days (immediate referral)

### Enterprise Sales

**Channel Description:** Direct sales to enterprise customers

**CAC Components:**
- Sales team: $300-500 (sales rep time)
- Sales tools: $50-100 (CRM, demos)
- Custom development: $100-200 (enterprise features)
- **Total CAC: $500-1,000**

**Conversion Flow:**
1. Sales outreach
2. Discovery call
3. Demo and evaluation
4. Proposal and negotiation
5. Contract signing

**Conversion Rate:** 10-20% (opportunity to close)

**Sales Cycle:** 60-180 days (longer enterprise cycle)

## Sales Efficiency Metrics

### Magic Number

**Definition:** Sales efficiency metric measuring revenue growth per dollar of sales and marketing spend

**Formula:**
```
Magic Number = (Net New ARR in Quarter) / (Sales & Marketing Spend in Previous Quarter)
```

**Targets:**
- < 0.5: Inefficient (spending too much)
- 0.5-0.75: Good (healthy growth)
- > 0.75: Excellent (efficient growth)

**Storytailor Target:** 0.6-0.8 (balanced growth and efficiency)

### Sales Efficiency Ratio

**Definition:** Ratio of revenue generated to sales and marketing investment

**Formula:**
```
Sales Efficiency Ratio = (Revenue Generated) / (Sales & Marketing Investment)
```

**Targets:**
- > 2:1: Efficient
- 1:1 to 2:1: Acceptable
- < 1:1: Inefficient

**Storytailor Target:** 2:1 to 3:1

### CAC Payback Period

**Definition:** Time to recover customer acquisition cost

**Formula:**
```
CAC Payback Period (months) = CAC / ARPU
```

**Targets:**
- < 3 months: Excellent
- 3-6 months: Good
- 6-12 months: Acceptable
- > 12 months: Poor

**Storytailor Targets:**
- PLG: < 2 months
- Paid Marketing: 3-4 months
- Enterprise: 6-12 months

**Code References:**
- `docs/business/unit-economics.md:287-319` - Payback period analysis

## Sales Team Economics

### Cost per Sales Rep

**Components:**
- Base salary: $60,000-100,000/year
- Commission: 10-20% of revenue
- Benefits: 20-30% of salary
- Tools: $5,000-10,000/year (CRM, sales tools)
- **Total Cost: $80,000-150,000/year**

### Quota Attainment

**Target Quota:** $50,000-100,000 ARR per rep per year

**Attainment Rates:**
- Top performers: 120-150% of quota
- Average performers: 80-100% of quota
- Underperformers: < 80% of quota

**Target:** 80% of reps at or above quota

### Ramp Time

**Definition:** Time for new sales rep to reach full productivity

**Ramp Timeline:**
- Month 1-2: Training and onboarding
- Month 3-4: First deals, 25-50% productivity
- Month 5-6: 50-75% productivity
- Month 7-12: Full productivity (100%)

**Target Ramp:** 6-9 months to full productivity

## Enterprise Sales Economics

### Enterprise CAC

**Components:**
- Sales team: $500-800 (longer sales cycle)
- Custom development: $200-400 (enterprise features)
- Legal/contracts: $50-100
- **Total CAC: $750-1,300**

### Enterprise Sales Cycle

**Typical Timeline:**
- Discovery: 2-4 weeks
- Evaluation: 4-8 weeks
- Proposal: 2-4 weeks
- Negotiation: 2-4 weeks
- Contract: 2-4 weeks
- **Total: 12-24 weeks (3-6 months)**

### Enterprise LTV

**Typical Enterprise Customer:**
- ARPU: $500-2,000/month
- Lifetime: 24-36 months
- **LTV: $12,000-72,000**

**LTV:CAC Ratio:** 10:1 to 20:1 (high value, high CAC)

## Conversion Rates by Stage and Channel

### PLG Free Tier Conversion Funnel

1. **Website Visit → Signup:** 10-20%
2. **Signup → First Story:** 60-80%
3. **First Story → Second Story:** 40-60%
4. **Second Story → Paid Conversion:** 5-10%
5. **Overall Conversion:** 0.1-0.5% (visit to paid)

### Paid Advertising Conversion Funnel

1. **Ad Click → Landing Page:** 80-90%
2. **Landing Page → Signup:** 5-15%
3. **Signup → Paid Conversion:** 2-5%
4. **Overall Conversion:** 0.1-0.7% (click to paid)

### Enterprise Sales Conversion Funnel

1. **Lead → Qualified Opportunity:** 20-30%
2. **Opportunity → Demo:** 60-80%
3. **Demo → Proposal:** 40-60%
4. **Proposal → Close:** 30-50%
5. **Overall Conversion:** 2-7% (lead to close)

## Sales Economics Optimization Strategies

### 1. Optimize PLG Channel

**Strategies:**
- Improve free tier experience (higher conversion)
- Reduce free tier costs (lower CAC)
- Increase viral mechanisms (referrals)
- Optimize upgrade prompts (better timing)

**Expected Impact:**
- CAC reduction: 20-30%
- Conversion rate increase: 10-20%

### 2. Scale Content Marketing

**Strategies:**
- Increase content production (more opportunities)
- Improve SEO (organic traffic)
- Content distribution (wider reach)
- Content optimization (higher conversion)

**Expected Impact:**
- CAC reduction: 10-20%
- Volume increase: 50-100%

### 3. Optimize Paid Advertising

**Strategies:**
- Improve targeting (higher conversion)
- Optimize landing pages (better conversion)
- A/B testing (continuous improvement)
- Retargeting campaigns (lower CAC)

**Expected Impact:**
- CAC reduction: 15-25%
- Conversion rate increase: 20-30%

### 4. Build Referral Program

**Strategies:**
- Incentivize referrals (customer acquisition)
- Make referral easy (reduce friction)
- Track and reward (program management)
- Viral mechanisms (network effects)

**Expected Impact:**
- CAC reduction: 40-50% (referral CAC)
- Volume increase: 20-30% (referral volume)

**Code References:**
- `docs/business/promotions/` - Referral campaigns

### 5. Develop Enterprise Sales

**Strategies:**
- Build sales team (dedicated enterprise)
- Create enterprise features (value proposition)
- Develop sales process (efficiency)
- Partner channels (lower CAC)

**Expected Impact:**
- Higher LTV: $5,000-20,000
- Higher margins: 70-80%
- Strategic value: Market positioning
