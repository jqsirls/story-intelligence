## Risk Factors and Mitigation

### Market Risks

**Risk:** Market education required (new category)

**Mitigation:**
- Content marketing (SEO, blog)
- Free tier (low barrier to entry)
- Quality demonstration (award-caliber stories)
- **Status:** Mitigated through PLG strategy

**Risk:** Competition from large tech companies

**Mitigation:**
- Technology moat (Story Intelligence™)
- Quality differentiation (award-caliber)
- First-mover advantage (market leadership)
- **Status:** Sustainable competitive advantage

### Financial Risks

**Risk:** Unit economics at scale (marginal costs)

**Mitigation:**
- Cost optimization (caching, batching)
- Usage limits (tier enforcement)
- Efficiency gains at scale
- **Status:** Optimized unit economics

**Risk:** Customer acquisition costs increase

**Mitigation:**
- PLG model (low CAC)
- Referral program (viral growth)
- Content marketing (sustainable)
- **Status:** Diversified acquisition channels

### Operational Risks

**Risk:** Infrastructure scaling costs

**Mitigation:**
- Serverless architecture (auto-scaling)
- Cost optimization (caching, efficiency)
- Reserved capacity (predictable workloads)
- **Status:** Scalable infrastructure

**Risk:** Team scaling challenges

**Mitigation:**
- Standardized processes
- Automation (reduce manual work)
- Culture and hiring (attract talent)
- **Status:** Scalable team structure
