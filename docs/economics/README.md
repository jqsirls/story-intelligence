# Economics Documentation

**Last Updated**: 2025-12-14  
**Audience**: Finance Team | Executive | Investors | Sales | Business Development

## Overview

This directory contains comprehensive economics documentation covering ROI analysis, sales economics, revenue models, financial projections, scaling economics, market economics, competitive economics, and investor materials. This documentation provides strategic financial analysis and decision-making support beyond operational finance metrics.

**Relationship to Other Documentation:**
- **Finance** (`docs/finance/`): Operational costs, metrics, and pricing - day-to-day financial operations
- **Business** (`docs/business/`): Strategy, operations, unit economics, pricing strategy - business operations
- **Economics** (`docs/economics/`): Strategic financial analysis, ROI, scaling, market economics - strategic decision-making
- **Sales** (`docs/sales/`): Sales process, playbooks, objections - sales operations
