## Market Sizing Methodology

### Bottom-Up Sizing

**Methodology:**
1. Define target customer segments
2. Estimate customers per segment
3. Estimate ARPU per segment
4. Calculate segment revenue
5. Sum segment revenues

**Example:**
- B2C Families: 1,000 customers × $15 ARPU × 12 months = $180,000/year
- B2B Education: 50 customers × $500 ARPU × 12 months = $300,000/year
- B2B Therapeutic: 100 customers × $30 ARPU × 12 months = $36,000/year
- **Total: $516,000/year**

### Top-Down Sizing

**Methodology:**
1. Define total market size (TAM)
2. Estimate serviceable portion (SAM)
3. Estimate obtainable portion (SOM)
4. Calculate market share
5. Calculate revenue

**Example:**
- US TAM: $5 billion/year
- SAM: $2 billion/year (40% serviceable)
- SOM: $100 million/year (5% obtainable, 5% of SAM)
- **Target Revenue: $100 million/year at 5% market share**
