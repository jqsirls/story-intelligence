## Objection Handling

### Objection: "My child is too young for stories"
**Response**: "Bedtime stories are designed for ages 2-8, with content automatically adapting to your child's age. For a 2-year-old, stories are very simple with repetitive, soothing language. As children grow, stories become slightly more complex while maintaining the calming, peaceful tone."

### Objection: "Will stories keep my child awake?"
**Response**: "Bedtime stories are specifically designed to promote sleep, not keep children awake. They use calming, repetitive language, peaceful themes, and endings that promote relaxation. Many parents report that bedtime stories actually help their children fall asleep faster."

### Objection: "I already read bedtime stories"
**Response**: "Our bedtime stories complement traditional reading. They're personalized to your child, adapt to their age, and are available on voice platforms—perfect for hands-free bedtime routines. Plus, you get unlimited stories, so you never run out of content."
