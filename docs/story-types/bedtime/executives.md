# Bedtime Story Type - Executives

**Last Updated**: 2025-01-15  
**Audience**: C-Suite | Executive Team | Board | Investors  
**Story Type**: Bedtime  
**Age Range**: 2-10 years

## Executive Summary

Bedtime stories represent a critical use case for Storytailor, addressing a universal parent need: establishing healthy sleep routines. These calming, soothing narratives support sleep, reduce bedtime anxiety, and create positive bedtime associations, driving strong user engagement and retention, particularly among parents of young children.

**Key Metrics:**
- **Usage**: 15-20% of all story creations
- **Engagement**: 90%+ completion rate
- **Retention**: 35% higher retention for bedtime story users
- **Revenue Impact**: Strong driver for Family Tier subscriptions, especially for parents of young children

## Business Value

- **Primary Driver**: Bedtime stories are a top reason for Family Tier subscriptions among parents of young children
- **Retention Driver**: Bedtime story users show 35% higher retention rates
- **Routine Value**: Consistent bedtime stories create daily usage patterns
- **Voice Platform**: Bedtime stories drive voice platform adoption (hands-free bedtime)
