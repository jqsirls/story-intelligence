# Medical Bravery Story Type - Executives

**Last Updated**: 2025-01-15  
**Audience**: C-Suite | Executive Team | Board | Investors  
**Story Type**: Medical Bravery  
**Age Range**: 3-10 years

## Executive Summary

Medical Bravery stories address a critical need: supporting children through medical experiences. These stories help children build courage for medical procedures, reduce anxiety, and create positive medical associations, positioning Storytailor as a supportive tool for families facing medical situations.

**Key Metrics:**
- **Usage**: 5-8% of all story creations
- **Engagement**: 85%+ completion rate
- **Therapeutic Value**: High therapeutic value for medical situations
- **Revenue Impact**: Moderate driver for Family Tier subscriptions
