# Medical Bravery Story Type - Sales

**Last Updated**: 2025-01-15  
**Audience**: Sales Team | Account Managers | Business Development  
**Story Type**: Medical Bravery  
**Age Range**: 3-10 years

## Sales Pitch

"Medical Bravery stories help children build courage for medical procedures through supportive narratives that provide accurate information, coping strategies, and positive medical experiences. Every story features characters facing medical situations with bravery, reducing anxiety and promoting positive medical experiences."

## Target Customer Profiles

### Profile 1: The Medical Parent
**Demographics:**
- Children ages 3-10
- Child facing medical procedures
- Needs medical preparation support
- Values reducing medical anxiety

**Pain Points:**
- Child is anxious about medical procedures
- Needs tools to prepare child for doctor visits
- Wants to reduce medical anxiety
- Seeks supportive medical content

**Solution:**
- Medical bravery stories that build courage
- Accurate but non-scary medical information
- Coping strategies for medical anxiety
- Positive medical experiences

**Tier**: Family Tier ($9.99/month)

### Profile 2: The Healthcare Provider
**Demographics:**
- Healthcare providers, child life specialists
- Works with children in medical settings
- Needs tools for medical preparation
- Values child support resources

**Pain Points:**
- Needs tools to prepare children for procedures
- Wants to reduce medical anxiety
- Seeks age-appropriate medical content
- Requires supportive medical resources

**Solution:**
- Medical bravery stories for procedure preparation
- Age-appropriate medical information
- Coping strategies for children
- Positive medical narratives

**Tier**: Professional Tier ($29.99/month) or Enterprise (custom)

## Pain Points Addressed

1. **"My child is scared of the doctor"**
   - Medical bravery stories build courage
   - Accurate but non-scary medical information
   - Coping strategies for medical anxiety

2. **"I need to prepare my child for surgery"**
   - Stories prepare children for medical procedures
   - Positive medical experiences
   - Healthcare workers as helpful heroes

## Key Selling Points

1. **Courage Building**: Stories help children build courage
2. **Anxiety Reduction**: Reduces medical anxiety
3. **Accurate Information**: Non-scary medical information
4. **Coping Strategies**: Teaches coping strategies
5. **Therapeutic Support**: Integrates with Therapeutic Agent
