# New Birth Story Type - Sales

**Last Updated**: 2025-01-15  
**Audience**: Sales Team | Account Managers | Business Development  
**Story Type**: New Birth (Adult Therapeutic)  
**Age Range**: Adult

## Sales Pitch

"New Birth stories celebrate welcoming new life and embracing change. These celebratory stories support new parents and families, acknowledging fears alongside excitement while celebrating life's precious moments and providing hope and guidance."

## Target Customer Profiles

### Profile 1: The New Parent
**Demographics:**
- New parents, expecting parents
- Families welcoming new life
- Needs support during transition
- Values emotional acknowledgment

**Pain Points:**
- Navigating new parenthood
- Complex emotions around new life
- Needs support and guidance
- Wants to celebrate while acknowledging challenges

**Solution:**
- New birth stories that celebrate and support
- Acknowledgment of complex emotions
- Hope and guidance for new beginnings
- Support for new parents

**Tier**: Family Tier ($9.99/month) or Professional Tier ($29.99/month)

## Key Selling Points

1. **New Life Celebration**: Stories celebrate new life and beginnings
2. **Parent Support**: Supports new parents and families
3. **Emotional Acknowledgment**: Acknowledges fears alongside excitement
4. **Hope and Guidance**: Provides hope and guidance for the future
5. **Therapeutic Support**: Integrates with Therapeutic Agent when needed
