# New Birth Story Type - Marketing

**Last Updated**: 2025-01-15  
**Audience**: Marketing Team | Content Team | Brand  
**Story Type**: New Birth (Adult Therapeutic)  
**Age Range**: Adult

## Value Proposition

New Birth stories celebrate welcoming new life and embracing change. These celebratory stories support new parents and families, acknowledging fears alongside excitement while celebrating life's precious moments.

## Target Audience

### Primary Users
- **Adults**: New parents, expecting parents, families welcoming new life
- **Therapists**: Mental health professionals supporting new parents
- **Support Groups**: New parent support groups

## Key Features and Benefits

### Core Features
- **New Life Celebration**: Joy and wonder of new beginnings
- **Transformation Themes**: Transformation and growth themes
- **Parent Support**: Support for new parents and families
- **Emotional Acknowledgment**: Acknowledgment of fears alongside excitement
- **Life Celebration**: Celebration of life's precious moments
- **Change Guidance**: Guidance for embracing change and responsibility
- **Hope and Optimism**: Hope and optimism for the future
- **Grounding**: Grounding in love, protection, and capability

### Benefits for Users
- Celebrates new life and beginnings
- Supports new parents
- Acknowledges complex emotions
- Provides hope and optimism
- Guides through change and responsibility

## Use Cases

### Common Scenarios
1. **New Parent Support**: Supporting new parents
2. **Birth Celebration**: Celebrating new birth
3. **Change Support**: Supporting through life changes
4. **Family Transition**: Supporting family transitions
5. **Emotional Support**: Acknowledging complex emotions around new life

## Messaging and Positioning

### Primary Message
"Celebrate new life and embrace change through stories that support new parents, acknowledge complex emotions, and provide hope and guidance."
