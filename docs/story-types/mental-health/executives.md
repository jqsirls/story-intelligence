# Mental Health Story Type - Executives

**Last Updated**: 2025-01-15  
**Audience**: C-Suite | Executive Team | Board | Investors  
**Story Type**: Mental Health  
**Age Range**: 3-12 years

## Executive Summary

Mental Health stories address the growing need for children's mental wellness support. These stories help children process emotions, learn coping strategies, and build emotional resilience, positioning Storytailor as a supportive tool for children's mental health.

**Key Metrics:**
- **Usage**: 8-12% of all story creations
- **Engagement**: 80%+ completion rate
- **Therapeutic Value**: High therapeutic value for emotional support
- **Revenue Impact**: Moderate driver for Family and Professional Tier subscriptions

## Business Value

### Revenue Impact
- **Family Tier**: Appeals to parents supporting children's mental wellness
- **Professional Tier**: Appeals to therapists and mental health professionals
- **Therapeutic Value**: High value for families needing emotional support
- **Retention**: Strong retention for mental health story users

## Market Opportunity

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
- **Children's Mental Health Market**: $2B+ annually
- **Therapeutic Content**: Growing segment
- **Emotional Support Tools**: Emerging market
- **EdTech Mental Health**: $500M+ market

## Revenue Potential

### Direct Revenue
- **Family Tier**: $9.99/month × 10% of users = $1.00/month per user
- **Professional Tier**: $29.99/month × 3% of users = $0.90/month per user
- **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**: $2.00-2.50/month for mental health story users

### Revenue Projections
- **Year 1**: 3K-15K users × $2.25 ARPU × 12 months = $81K-$405K
- **Year 3**: 30K-150K users × $2.25 ARPU × 12 months = $810K-$4.05M
