# Mental Health Story Type - Sales

**Last Updated**: 2025-01-15  
**Audience**: Sales Team | Account Managers | Business Development  
**Story Type**: Mental Health  
**Age Range**: 3-12 years

## Sales Pitch

"Mental Health stories support children's emotional well-being through narratives that help them process emotions, learn coping strategies, and build emotional resilience. Every story addresses emotions and feelings in healthy ways, building self-awareness and emotional intelligence."

## Target Customer Profiles

### Profile 1: The Emotion-Support Parent
**Demographics:**
- Children ages 4-12
- Child needs emotional support
- Values mental wellness
- Seeks tools for emotional development

**Pain Points:**
- Child struggles with emotions
- Needs tools to teach coping strategies
- Wants to support emotional development
- Seeks age-appropriate mental health content

**Solution:**
- Mental health stories that support emotions
- Coping strategies through narratives
- Emotional resilience building
- Age-appropriate mental wellness content

**Tier**: Family Tier ($9.99/month) or Professional Tier ($29.99/month)

### Profile 2: The Therapist
**Demographics:**
- Child therapists, counselors
- Works with children ages 4-12
- Needs therapeutic tools
- Values evidence-based approaches

**Pain Points:**
- Needs therapeutic stories for children
- Wants age-appropriate mental health content
- Seeks tools for emotional support
- Requires therapeutic integration

**Solution:**
- Mental health stories with therapeutic support
- Therapeutic Agent integration
- Evidence-based coping strategies
- Professional referral pathways

**Tier**: Professional Tier ($29.99/month) or Enterprise (custom)

## Pain Points Addressed

1. **"My child struggles with emotions"**
   - Mental health stories help process emotions
   - Coping strategies through narratives
   - Emotional validation and support

2. **"I need tools to teach coping skills"**
   - Stories teach coping strategies
   - Age-appropriate mental health content
   - Therapeutic integration available

## Key Selling Points

1. **Emotional Support**: Stories support emotional well-being
2. **Coping Strategies**: Teaches coping strategies
3. **Therapeutic Integration**: Integrates with Therapeutic Agent
4. **Age-Appropriate**: Stories adapt to child's age (4-12)
5. **Resilience Building**: Builds emotional resilience
