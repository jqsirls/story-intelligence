# Educational Story Type - Executives

**Last Updated**: 2025-01-15  
**Audience**: C-Suite | Executive Team | Board | Investors  
**Story Type**: Educational  
**Age Range**: 3-12 years

## Executive Summary

Educational stories represent a significant market opportunity, addressing the $2.5B+ children's educational content market. These learning-focused narratives integrate with Educational Agent for curriculum alignment, learning objectives, and assessment features, positioning Storytailor as a comprehensive educational tool for both home and classroom use.

**Key Metrics:**
- **Usage**: 20-25% of all story creations
- **Engagement**: 80%+ completion rate
- **Educational Market**: $2.5B+ annual market
- **Revenue Impact**: Strong driver for Professional Tier subscriptions
