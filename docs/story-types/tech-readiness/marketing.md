# Tech Readiness Story Type - Marketing

**Last Updated**: 2025-01-15  
**Audience**: Marketing Team | Content Team | Brand  
**Story Type**: Tech Readiness  
**Age Range**: 3-14 years

## Value Proposition

Tech Readiness stories introduce technology concepts, digital citizenship, and online safety through age-appropriate narratives. These stories help children understand technology, use it safely and responsibly, and develop positive digital citizenship habits.

## Target Audience

### Primary Users
- **Children Ages 6-14**: Children learning about technology
- **Parents**: Teaching digital literacy and online safety
- **Educators**: Teaching technology concepts in school
- **Digital Literacy Educators**: Using stories to teach digital skills

## Key Features and Benefits

### Core Features
- **Age-Appropriate Tech Concepts**: Introduction to digital concepts
- **Safe Technology Use**: Safe and responsible technology use
- **Problem-Solving**: Characters using technology to solve problems
- **Digital Balance**: Balance between digital and real-world activities
- **Digital Citizenship**: Positive role models for digital citizenship

### Benefits for Children
- Learn about technology safely
- Understand digital citizenship
- Develop responsible technology habits
- Learn online safety
- Build digital literacy skills

## Use Cases

### Common Scenarios
1. **Digital Literacy**: Teaching technology concepts
2. **Online Safety**: Teaching online safety and digital citizenship
3. **Technology Education**: Introducing technology in educational settings
4. **Digital Balance**: Teaching balance between digital and real-world
5. **Tech Concepts**: Learning about computers, internet, coding

## Messaging and Positioning

### Primary Message
"Teach technology concepts and digital citizenship through stories that introduce technology safely, promote responsible use, and build digital literacy skills."
