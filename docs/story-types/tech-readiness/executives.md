# Tech Readiness Story Type - Executives

**Last Updated**: 2025-01-15  
**Audience**: C-Suite | Executive Team | Board | Investors  
**Story Type**: Tech Readiness  
**Age Range**: 3-14 years

## Executive Summary

Tech Readiness stories address the growing need for early digital literacy education. These stories introduce technology concepts, digital citizenship, and online safety, positioning Storytailor in the digital literacy education market.

**Key Metrics:**
- **Usage**: 5-10% of all story creations
- **Engagement**: 75%+ completion rate
- **Market Opportunity**: Growing digital literacy education market
- **Revenue Impact**: Moderate driver for Family and Professional Tier subscriptions

## Business Value

### Revenue Impact
- **Family Tier**: Appeals to parents teaching digital literacy
- **Professional Tier**: Appeals to educators teaching technology
- **Market Differentiation**: Unique story-based digital literacy approach
