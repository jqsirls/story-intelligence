# Tech Readiness Story Type - Sales

**Last Updated**: 2025-01-15  
**Audience**: Sales Team | Account Managers | Business Development  
**Story Type**: Tech Readiness  
**Age Range**: 3-14 years

## Sales Pitch

"Tech Readiness stories teach technology concepts and digital citizenship through engaging narratives that introduce technology safely, promote responsible use, and build digital literacy skills. Every story helps children understand technology and use it safely and responsibly."

## Target Customer Profiles

### Profile 1: The Digital Literacy Parent
**Demographics:**
- Children ages 6-14
- Values digital literacy education
- Wants to teach online safety
- Seeks age-appropriate technology content

**Pain Points:**
- Needs to teach technology concepts
- Wants age-appropriate digital content
- Seeks tools for online safety education
- Values responsible technology use

**Solution:**
- Tech readiness stories with digital citizenship
- Age-appropriate technology concepts
- Online safety education
- Responsible technology use

**Tier**: Family Tier ($9.99/month) or Professional Tier ($29.99/month)

## Key Selling Points

1. **Digital Literacy**: Stories teach technology concepts
2. **Online Safety**: Promotes safe and responsible technology use
3. **Digital Citizenship**: Builds positive digital citizenship habits
4. **Age-Appropriate**: Stories adapt to child's age (6-14)
5. **Educational Value**: Integrates with educational objectives
