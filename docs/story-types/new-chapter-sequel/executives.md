# New Chapter Sequel Story Type - Executives

**Last Updated**: 2025-01-15  
**Audience**: C-Suite | Executive Team | Board | Investors  
**Story Type**: New Chapter Sequel  
**Age Range**: 3-12 years

## Executive Summary

New Chapter Sequel stories continue adventures with existing characters, creating ongoing story series that drive engagement and retention. These stories maintain character consistency while introducing fresh challenges, building long-term user relationships.

**Key Metrics:**
- **Usage**: 10-15% of all story creations
- **Engagement**: 90%+ completion rate
- **Retention**: 50% higher retention for sequel story users
- **Revenue Impact**: Strong driver for engagement and retention

## Business Value

### Revenue Impact
