## Overview

This document describes the complete partnership lifecycle from discovery and qualification through onboarding, co-marketing, success measurement, and renewal/expansion.

## Discovery and Qualification

### Partner Discovery

**Discovery Channels:**
- Partner applications via website
- Referrals from existing partners
- Industry events and conferences
- Direct outreach by Business Development team
- Inbound inquiries from potential partners

**Initial Contact:**
- Partner application review
- Initial discovery call
- Needs assessment
- Partnership fit evaluation

### Qualification Process

**Technical Qualification:**
- Technical capabilities assessment
- Integration complexity evaluation
- Development team evaluation
- Platform compatibility review

**Business Qualification:**
- Market presence and reach
- Customer base and target market
- Revenue potential
- Strategic alignment
- Financial stability

**Qualification Criteria:**
- **Technology Partners**: Development experience, API integration skills, customer base
- **Solution Partners**: Established business, sales capabilities, support infrastructure
- **Strategic Partners**: Significant market reach, strategic fit, growth potential

### Partnership Fit Assessment

**Fit Factors:**
- Market alignment
- Technical compatibility
- Strategic value
- Revenue potential
- Cultural fit

**Assessment Process:**
- Internal review by Business Development team
- Technical review by Engineering team
- Strategic review by Executive team
- Decision on partnership type and terms

## Partnership Health Monitoring

### Health Indicators

**Positive Indicators:**
- Growing revenue
- Increasing customer acquisition
- High integration adoption
- Strong partner satisfaction
- Active co-marketing

**Warning Signs:**
- Declining revenue
- Low customer acquisition
- Low integration adoption
- Partner dissatisfaction
- Inactive partnership

### Intervention Strategies

**Support Strategies:**
- Additional technical support
- Marketing assistance
- Sales enablement
- Training and resources

**Escalation:**
- Executive engagement
- Strategic review
- Partnership restructuring
- Termination (if necessary)
