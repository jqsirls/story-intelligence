## Partnership Program Overview

The Storytailor Partnership Program enables organizations to integrate, resell, white-label, or collaborate on custom solutions. Partnerships range from technology integrations to strategic enterprise deployments.

### Partnership Objectives

**For Partners:**
- Integrate Story Intelligence™ into existing products
- Resell Storytailor services to customers
- White-label Story Intelligence™ technology
- Build custom solutions for enterprise customers

**For Storytailor:**
- Expand market reach through partner network
- Enable integrations with complementary platforms
- Support enterprise customer needs
- Build strategic relationships

## Partnership Types

### Technology Partnerships

- **App Developers**: Integrate storytelling into mobile/web apps
- **Platform Providers**: Add storytelling to existing platforms
- **Voice Developers**: Create voice-first storytelling experiences
- **EdTech Companies**: Educational storytelling solutions

### Solution Partnerships

- **Agencies**: Offer storytelling solutions to clients
- **Consultants**: Implementation and customization services
- **Resellers**: Distribute Storytailor to end customers
- **System Integrators**: Enterprise deployment specialists

### Strategic Partnerships

- **Enterprise Customers**: Large-scale custom implementations
- **OEM Partners**: Embed storytelling in hardware/software products
- **Channel Partners**: Distribution through existing channels
- **Platform Partners**: Deep integrations with major platforms
