# Affiliate Program

**Last Updated**: 2025-12-14  
**Audience**: Business Development | Affiliates | Marketing  
**Status**: ✅ Production Ready

## Overview

The Storytailor Affiliate Program enables partners to earn commissions by referring customers to Storytailor. Affiliates receive revenue share based on successful referrals and conversions.

## Program Benefits

### For Affiliates

- **Earn Commissions**: 20-30% commission on referred customer subscriptions
- **Recurring Revenue**: Earn on subscription renewals (first year)
- **Marketing Support**: Access to marketing materials, banners, and resources
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Monitor conversions and earnings in real-time
- **Flexible Payouts**: Monthly payouts via Stripe or bank transfer
- **Dedicated Support**: Access to affiliate support team

### For Storytailor

- **Customer Acquisition**: Reach new audiences through affiliate network
- **Brand Awareness**: Expand brand visibility through affiliate promotions
- **Cost-Effective Marketing**: Pay only for successful conversions
- **Strategic Partnerships**: Build relationships with content creators and influencers

## Eligibility Requirements

### Standard Affiliate

**Requirements:**
- Active website, blog, or social media presence
- Relevant audience (parents, educators, children's content creators)
- Minimum 1,000 followers/subscribers
- No competing products or services
- Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

**Benefits:**
- 20% commission on first-year revenue
- Access to marketing materials
- Real-time tracking dashboard
- Monthly payouts

### Premium Affiliate

**Requirements:**
- Active website, blog, or social media presence
- Relevant audience (parents, educators, children's content creators)
- Minimum 10,000 followers/subscribers
- Established content creation track record
- No competing products or services
- Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

**Benefits:**
- 25% commission on first-year revenue
- Priority marketing support
- Custom marketing materials
- Real-time tracking dashboard
- Monthly payouts
- Dedicated affiliate manager

### Enterprise Affiliate

**Requirements:**
- Established business or organization
- Significant audience reach (50,000+)
- Strategic partnership potential
- No competing products or services
- Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

**Benefits:**
- 30% commission on first-year revenue (or custom structure)
- Custom marketing materials and campaigns
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Real-time tracking dashboard
- Flexible payout terms
- Dedicated partnership manager

## Commission Rates

### Subscription Commissions

| Affiliate Tier | Commission Rate | Minimum Payout |
|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| Standard | 20% of first-year revenue | $50 |
| Premium | 25% of first-year revenue | $50 |
| Enterprise | 30% of first-year revenue (or custom) | $100 |

### Commission Details

- **Base Commission**: Percentage of subscription revenue
- **Recurring Commissions**: Earn on subscription renewals (first year only)
- **Commission Period**: First 12 months of subscription
- **One-Time Purchases**: 10% commission on story pack purchases
- **Enterprise Deals**: Custom commission structure negotiated

### Commission Calculation Example

**Example:**
- Affiliate refers customer who signs up for Family Tier ($9.99/month)
- Commission rate: 20%
- Monthly commission: $9.99 × 20% = $1.998/month
- Annual commission (first year): $1.998 × 12 = $23.98

## Terms and Conditions

### Affiliate Responsibilities

**Marketing Standards:**
- Promote Storytailor accurately and ethically
- Use approved marketing materials and messaging
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- Maintain brand guidelines and messaging standards
- Do not engage in spam or unethical marketing practices

**Reporting:**
- Report any issues or concerns promptly
- Provide accurate information in application
- Update contact information as needed
- Respond to communications from Storytailor team

### Program Rules

**Account Management:**
- One affiliate account per person/organization
- Referral codes cannot be shared with other affiliates
- Affiliate accounts are non-transferable
- Storytailor reserves right to modify program terms

**Commission Rules:**
- Commissions only paid on legitimate referrals
- Self-referrals are not eligible for commissions
- Fraudulent activity results in account termination
- Commissions subject to verification and approval

**Termination:**
- Storytailor reserves right to terminate affiliate accounts
- Termination may occur for violation of terms, fraudulent activity, or other reasons
- Outstanding commissions paid per payout schedule
- Affiliate may terminate account at any time

### Compliance

**Legal Requirements:**
- Affiliates must comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- FTC disclosure requirements for affiliate links
- COPPA compliance for child-focused content
- GDPR compliance for EU audiences

- `docs/compliance/coppa.md` - COPPA compliance requirements
