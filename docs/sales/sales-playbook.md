## üéØ Sales Process

### 1. Discovery
**Goal**: Understand prospect's needs and pain points

**Key Questions**:
- What age are your children?
- What challenges do you face with bedtime stories/educational content?
- Are you looking for therapeutic support?
- What platforms do you use? (web, mobile, voice)

**Pain Points to Identify**:
- Lack of personalized content
- Time constraints for story creation
- Need for age-appropriate material
- Therapeutic or educational requirements

### 2. Demo
**Goal**: Show Story Intelligence‚Ñ¢ in action

**Demo Flow**:
1. **Show Story Creation** (2-3 minutes)
   - Create a character
   - Generate a personalized story
   - Highlight age-appropriateness

2. **Show Personalization** (2 minutes)
   - Demonstrate how stories adapt to child's age
   - Show different story types
   - Highlight therapeutic options

3. **Show Multi-Platform** (1 minute)
   - Web interface
   - Mobile app preview
   - Voice assistant integration

4. **Show Safety Features** (1 minute)
   - COPPA compliance
   - Content moderation
   - Parent controls

**Total Demo Time**: 6-7 minutes

See [Objection Handling Guide](./sales-objections.md)

- Reference: [`docs/brand/creator-positioning-jq-sirls.md`](docs/brand/creator-positioning-jq-sirls.md) for complete creator story

### 4. Closing
**Goal**: Convert to paid subscription

**Closing Techniques**:
- **Trial Offer**: "Try Family Tier free for 7 days"

- **Social Proof**: "Join 10,000+ families creating personalized stories"

### Free Tier
- **Price**: $0/month
- **Best For**: Trying the platform
- **Limitations**: 5 stories/month, web only
- **Upsell Path**: "Unlock unlimited stories and all platforms"

### Family Tier
- **Price**: $9.99/month
- **Best For**: Most families
- **Value**: Unlimited stories, all platforms, Kid Intelligence
- **ROI**: "Less than a coffee per week for unlimited personalized stories"

### Professional Tier
- **Price**: $29.99/month
- **Best For**: Educators, therapists, power users
- **Value**: Multiple profiles, analytics, API access
- **ROI**: "Professional tools for less than a therapy session"

### Enterprise Tier
- **Price**: Custom
- **Best For**: Schools, organizations, large families
- **Value**: Custom integrations, dedicated support, volume discounts
- **ROI**: "Bulk pricing and custom solutions"
