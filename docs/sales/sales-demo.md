## üéØ Demo Tips

### Do's ‚úÖ
- ‚úÖ **Practice**: Know the demo flow cold
- ‚úÖ **Personalize**: Use their child's name/age in demo
- ‚úÖ **Engage**: Ask questions, check understanding
- ‚úÖ **Pause**: Give them time to process
- ‚úÖ **Listen**: Watch for reactions and adjust

### Don'ts ‚ùå
- ‚ùå **Rush**: Take your time, let them absorb
- ‚ùå **Overwhelm**: Focus on key features
- ‚ùå **Assume**: Don't assume they understand
- ‚ùå **Ignore Questions**: Address questions immediately
- ‚ùå **Skip Safety**: Always cover safety features

## üõ†Ô∏è Demo Preparation

### Before Demo
1. **Test Environment**: Verify demo account works
2. **Sample Stories**: Have pre-generated examples ready
3. **Backup Plan**: Have screenshots if live demo fails
4. **Materials**: Pricing sheet, feature list ready

### During Demo
1. **Screen Share**: Use clear screen sharing
2. **Audio**: Ensure good audio quality
3. **Recording**: Ask permission to record (for follow-up)
4. **Notes**: Take notes on questions/concerns

### After Demo
1. **Follow Up**: Send demo recording and materials
2. **Answer Questions**: Address any unanswered questions
3. **Next Steps**: Schedule follow-up or close
4. **Thank You**: Send thank you note
