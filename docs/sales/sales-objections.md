### 1. Listen
- Understand the real concern
- Don't interrupt
- Ask clarifying questions

### 2. Acknowledge
- Validate their concern
- Show empathy
- "I understand that's important to you"

### 3. Address
- Provide specific response
- Use data/stories when possible
- Reference relevant features

### 4. Confirm
- Check if concern is resolved
- Ask if they have other questions
- Move toward next step
