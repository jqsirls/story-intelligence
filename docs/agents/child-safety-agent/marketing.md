# Child Safety Agent - Marketing Information

**Status**: Draft

## Value Proposition

The Child Safety Agent is Storytailor's **comprehensive child safety system** that provides crisis detection, mandatory reporting, and professional escalation to ensure child safety during all platform interactions. It gives parents peace of mind while maintaining privacy and compliance.

## Key Features and Benefits

### Crisis Detection
- **Real-time Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring for signs of distress, abuse, or self-harm
- **Voice Pattern Analysis**: Detects emotional distress through voice patterns
- **Interaction Monitoring**: Monitors conversations for concerning patterns
- **Risk Assessment**: Multi-level risk scoring with safety planning

### Mandatory Reporting
- **Automated Reporting**: Automated reporting to appropriate authorities
- **Legal Compliance**: Complies with state and federal reporting requirements
- **Jurisdiction Support**: Jurisdiction-specific reporting protocols
- **Audit Trail**: Complete audit trail for compliance

### Parent Notifications
- **Immediate Alerts**: Immediate alerts to parents/guardians for safety concerns
- **Privacy Compliant**: Privacy-compliant notification system
- **Escalation Protocols**: Multi-level escalation for different risk levels
- **Follow-up Support**: Follow-up monitoring and support
