# Conversation Agent - Marketing

**Last Updated**: 2025-12-14

## Value Proposition

The Conversation Agent enables real-time, empathetic voice conversations that create award-caliber personal stories through emotionally intelligent dialogue. It's the heart of Storytailor's unique value proposition—transforming story generation into emotionally intelligent companionship.

## Key Features

### Real-Time Voice Conversations

**What It Is**:
- Bidirectional real-time communication via ElevenLabs Conversational AI
- Natural conversation flow with <800ms response time
- WebSocket-based for seamless interaction

**Why It Matters**:
- Creates authentic, engaging experiences
- Feels like talking to a friend, not a machine
- Enables natural storytelling collaboration

**Marketing Message**:
"Talk to Frankie like a friend. Real-time voice conversations that feel natural and engaging, creating stories together through dialogue."

### Emotion-Aware Storytelling

**What It Is**:
- 3-tier emotion handling system (everyday feelings, big feelings, concerning cues)
- Emotion detection and appropriate response
- Integration of emotions into story narrative

**Why It Matters**:
- Helps children process emotions through storytelling
- Creates emotionally safe space for expression
- Builds emotional intelligence and regulation skills

**Marketing Message**:
"Stories that understand feelings. Frankie recognizes and responds to your child's emotions, creating stories that help them process and understand their feelings."

### Age-Adaptive Interactions

**What It Is**:
- Adjusts vocabulary, sentence length, and choice complexity to match developmental stage
- Age-specific conversation styles (3-4, 5-6, 7-9, 10+)
- Honors sophistication while keeping interactions playful

**Why It Matters**:
- Ensures age-appropriate engagement
- Builds confidence through appropriate challenges
- Grows with your child

**Marketing Message**:
"Grows with your child. Frankie adapts to your child's age and developmental stage, ensuring every conversation is just right."

### Smart Home Integration

**What It Is**:
- Dynamic Philips Hue lighting that responds to conversation context
- Emotion-based lighting changes
- Story-specific lighting (adventure, bedtime, educational)

**Why It Matters**:
- Creates immersive storytelling experiences
- Enhances emotional connection through ambient lighting
- Makes stories come alive in the room

**Marketing Message**:
"Stories that light up the room. Dynamic lighting that responds to your child's emotions and story choices, creating immersive storytelling experiences."

### Accessibility Support

**What It Is**:
- Specialized handling for AAC, stuttering, bilingual code-switching, unclear speech
- Multiple ways to participate (voice, choice selection, pointing)
- Adapts to individual communication styles

**Why It Matters**:
- Inclusive design for all children
- No child left behind
- Builds confidence for children with communication differences

**Marketing Message**:
"Stories for everyone. Frankie adapts to your child's communication style, whether they speak clearly, stutter, use AAC, or code-switch between languages."

- Calming story themes
- Gradual fade-out for sleep

**Marketing Message**:

**Scenario**: Child expresses big feelings

"Stories that help your child process emotions. When your child expresses big feelings, Frankie responds with empathy and support."

**Scenario**: Child wants to learn through storytelling
