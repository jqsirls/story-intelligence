## Value Proposition

Fieldnotes is a standing internal research team that never sleeps, providing adversarial truth-telling insights through five parallel analysis tracks. Currently for internal use only, with future productization planned.

## Key Features

### Continuous Research Team

- **Never sleeps** - 24/7 observation and analysis
- **Unbiased** - Data-driven, not opinion-driven
- **Adversarial** - Challenges assumptions and detects self-deception
- **Integrated** - Delivers insights in your workflow (Slack, Email, Webhooks)

### Five Parallel Tracks

1. **Continuous Insight Mining** - Watches product usage, abandoned flows, retries, rage clicks
2. **Buyer Reality Checks** - Simulates and pressure-tests buyer/decision-maker expectations
3. **User Experience Guardrails** - Evaluates outcomes through end-user lens
4. **Concept Interrogation** - Pre-mortem analysis before building features
5. **Brand Consistency** - Audits language, onboarding, and flows against brand voice

### Integration-Native Design

**No dashboard to check. No login required. Just insights in your workflow.**

- **Slack** - Weekly briefs and critical findings
- **Email** - Scheduled reports and alerts
- **Webhooks** - Custom integrations (Make.com, Zapier, Buildship)
- **API** - Programmatic access for internal tools

### Cost-Efficient

- **Internal use**: $150-300/month
- **90% cheaper** than traditional research consultants ($10K-50K/month)
- **Continuous insights** (not one-time studies)
- **High margin potential** for future external customers (73-83%)
