## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Twitter/X

**Platform Characteristics:**
- Fast-paced, real-time
- Character limit: 280 characters
- Thread capability for longer content
- Hashtag usage common

**Content Types:**
- Quick updates and announcements
- Story highlights and examples
- Industry insights and tips
- Customer testimonials (with permission)
- Threads for longer-form content

**Posting Frequency:**
- 3-5 posts per week
- Mix of original content and engagement

**Best Times:**
- Weekdays: 9-11 AM, 1-3 PM, 5-7 PM
- Weekends: 10 AM-12 PM

**Voice and Tone:**
- Concise and engaging
- Warm but professional
- Use Story Intelligence™ messaging
- Avoid jargon, keep it simple

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- Primary: #StoryIntelligence, #Storytailor
- Secondary: #PersonalizedStories, #FamilyTime, #ChildDevelopment
- Use 2-3 hashtags per post (don't overuse)

**Code References:**
- `docs/design/voice-and-tone.md` - Voice guidelines

### LinkedIn

**Platform Characteristics:**
- Professional network
- Longer-form content supported
- Article publishing available
- B2B and professional focus

**Content Types:**
- Industry insights and thought leadership
- Educational content (child development, storytelling)
- Company updates and milestones
- Professional use cases (educators, therapists)
- Long-form articles (LinkedIn Articles)

**Posting Frequency:**

**Best Times:**

**Voice and Tone:**

- Evidence-based and credible
- Educational and informative
- Avoid overly casual language

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- Professional: #ChildDevelopment, #EdTech, #TherapeuticStorytelling
- Industry: #FamilyTech, #PersonalizedContent, #StoryIntelligence
- Use 3-5 hashtags per post

## Engagement Guidelines

### How to Respond

**Response Time Standards:**
- Comments: Within 24 hours (ideally within 4-6 hours)
- Direct messages: Within 24 hours
- Mentions: Within 12 hours
- Crisis situations: Immediate response

**Response Tone:**
- Warm and helpful (customer support tone)
- Authentic and genuine (not templated)
- Empathetic (acknowledge concerns)
- Professional (maintain brand voice)

**Code References:**
- `docs/design/voice-and-tone.md:47-56` - Customer support tone
- `docs/design/communication-standards.md` - Communication standards

### Engagement Best Practices

**Do's:**
- ✅ Respond to all comments and questions
- ✅ Acknowledge positive feedback (thank customers)
- ✅ Address concerns promptly and empathetically
- ✅ Engage authentically (not just promotional)
- ✅ Share user-generated content (with permission)
- ✅ Participate in relevant conversations

**Don'ts:**
- ❌ Ignore negative comments (address them)
- ❌ Delete negative comments (unless abusive)
- ❌ Use templated responses (be authentic)
- ❌ Over-promote (balance content)
- ❌ Engage in arguments (stay professional)

## Story Intelligence Representation on Social Media

### How to Represent Story Intelligence™

**Always:**
- Use ™ symbol
- Capitalize both words
- Use as proper noun
- Explain value, not just technology

**Example:**
- ✅ "Story Intelligence™ creates award-caliber stories that are uniquely tailored to each child."
- ❌ "Our AI-powered story intelligence technology generates personalized content." (not capitalized, no ™, too technical)

**Code References:**
- `docs/brand/01-story-intelligence-brand-guide.md:203-211` - Story Intelligence language

### Story Intelligence Messaging

**What We Say:**
- "Story Intelligence™ crafts award-caliber narratives"
- "Powered by Story Intelligence™"
- "Story Intelligence understands your child"
- "Story Intelligence creates family treasures"

**What We Never Say:**
- "Story Intelligence™ powered" or "Story Intelligence™ enhanced"
- "AI-generated" or "machine learning"
- "Automated" or "algorithmic"

## Crisis Communication on Social Media

### Crisis Response Protocol

**Immediate Response (Within 1 Hour):**
1. Acknowledge the issue publicly
2. Express empathy and concern
3. Commit to resolution
4. Provide contact information for direct communication

**Example:**
"We're aware of [issue] and take this seriously. We're investigating immediately and will provide an update within [timeframe]. Please contact us directly at [contact] for immediate assistance."

**Follow-Up:**
- Provide regular updates (every 4-6 hours during active crisis)
- Be transparent about resolution progress
- Apologize if appropriate
- Share resolution and prevention measures

### Crisis Communication Principles

**Do's:**
- ✅ Respond quickly (within 1 hour)
- ✅ Be transparent and honest
- ✅ Take responsibility (if appropriate)
- ✅ Provide regular updates
- ✅ Show empathy and concern

**Don'ts:**
- ❌ Ignore or delete crisis-related posts
- ❌ Be defensive or dismissive
- ❌ Make promises you can't keep
- ❌ Blame others (take responsibility)
- ❌ Delay response unnecessarily

## Social Media Do's and Don'ts

### Do's

**✅ Do:**
- Use Story Intelligence™ correctly (™ symbol, capitalization)

- Engage authentically (not templated)
- Respond promptly (within 24 hours)

## Content Approval Process

### Approval Workflow

**Standard Content:**
1. Content creator drafts post
2. Review against voice/tone guidelines
3. Review against messaging framework
4. Approve and schedule/publish

**Sensitive Content:**
1. Content creator drafts post
2. Review against guidelines
3. Additional review (marketing lead, legal if needed)
4. Approve and schedule/publish

**Crisis Content:**
1. Immediate draft (crisis response)
2. Quick review (marketing lead, executive if needed)
3. Immediate publish (time-sensitive)

### Approval Criteria

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Voice and tone appropriate
- [ ] Messaging aligns with framework
- [ ] Story Intelligence™ used correctly
- [ ] No inappropriate content
- [ ] Hashtags appropriate
- [ ] Visual content high quality
- [ ] No privacy violations
- [ ] Platform-appropriate format

## Social Media Metrics and KPIs

### Key Metrics

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Likes, comments, shares
- Engagement rate (engagement / followers)
- Reach and impressions
- Click-through rate (CTR)

**Growth Metrics:**
- Follower growth rate
- New followers per month
- Audience demographics
- Follower quality (engagement rate)

**Content Metrics:**
- Top performing content
- Content type performance
- Posting time performance
- Hashtag performance

### Target KPIs

**Engagement Rate:**
- Target: 3-5% (industry average: 1-3%)
- Measure: (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Followers × 100%

**Follower Growth:**
- Target: 5-10% monthly growth
- Measure: (New Followers - Lost Followers) / Starting Followers × 100%

**Reach:**
- Target: 2-3x follower count (organic reach)
- Measure: Total reach / Followers

**Click-Through Rate:**
- Target: 2-5% (varies by platform)
- Measure: Clicks / Impressions × 100%
